Raising awereness and Sailing the Fastnet

Those of you who know us might know that we have a little neece that is suffering from a rare genetic defect. We have decided to start a fund raiser and join the most infamous regatta in the world - The Fastnet!

My little neece is the sweetest thing that I have ever met. She is happy as a person can be, I can honestly say her laughter is the most contagous thing on this planet. She was born with a rare condition, called the "22q13.3 Deletion Syndrome", sometimes also referred to as Phelan-McDermid Syndrome. To raise awareness and support continued research into the fight against Phelan-McDermid we have decided to run a fund raiser. To challenge ourselves we will join the most infamous sailing race in the world - the Rolex Fastnet Race. We have not figured out all the details yet, but if you would like to help out in any way - please get in touch!
