Help us raise awereness for Phelan-McDermid Syndrome

Phelan-McDermid Syndrome (also called 22q13.3 Deletion Syndrome) is a rare genetic defect. Across the globe, only around 1600 cases are documented. We are trying to raise awareness and money that can go into research. Even if 22q13.3 can not be beat, a lot can be done for people suffering from it.
